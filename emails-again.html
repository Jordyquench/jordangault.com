REMUS UOMO EMAILS

I finally got to try my hand at doing the Remus emails this week. Before that though I was back to the danske emails after doing my heart loader. I wasn't too bothered about this because I do like doing the emails when it isn't the only thing I am doing.

So onto the the Remus email, I had seen how they were meant to be done but this was the first time I was getting to do them myself. I think I mentioned in a previous post that they are done using Mailchimp. I had heard Paul and Kyle talk about Mailchimp in Uni but this was the first time I had used it. The first time I opened it, like anything I needed to find my way around it, but to be honest it didn't take too long, it has a very simple interface and once I used it for about 30 minutes to an hour I felt pretty comfortable.

I have to admit, doing the remus emails is a lot less time consuming than the Danske ones, I think it is becasue the layout for them is very similar each week, All I am doing is copying the images they send over in the email and placing them within masks on sketch, adjusting a few small things and changing the text them adding all of that data into Mailchimp and we are good to go.
