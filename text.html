THE CONVERSATIONS

I've got myself another project for Danske Discovery, this time it is more animations. They asked could we do this one and again it came to me because I have done a few animations now. These are for instagram ads to try and promote Danske Discovery current accounts. They want me to create animated text conversations between a parent and their child who is looking to to set up an account.

After the last animation project I finshed, I now know that when doing these I can create everything I need on Illustrator first and then import that into after effects to add the animations. So I designed a phone text screen with all of the text bubbles using Danske Discoverys' colour schemes and Danskes' fonts.

Once I got them into after effects I tried to make them look like what a text conversation would look like on a phone and it turned out pretty good, to start with some of the messages were hard to read but that was just because of the copy that Danske supplied me. They went back and shortened it and it made it a lot easier to read.

They ended up coming back and wanting some more conversations done becasue they really liked the first one. To make them I just had to go in and change the size of the bubbles and change the text, it was pretty simple once I got the first one finshed.

THE OUTCOME

The two conversations turned out really well in my opinion. There was a few different verisons that they wanted me to try out, the first one I did had all of the texts typing in, but they wanted me to try it without that feature and then for the typing to just happen on the side of the person whos phone we are looking at.

Once I got this fixed and the timing down it was simply changing the copy and making sure everything lines up. The time constraints for instgram ad videos was something that at the start it made me worry about fitting everything in but once they said about changing the typing effect it made a big difference in fitting it all in.

I still though the copy was a bit long though so we went back to the and told them this and they chanhed it so that it was shorter and again it worked really well. now you can read it all without really trying to read quick.
