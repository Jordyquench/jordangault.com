BRIEF

The brief for this project was basically to create a weather app, it was very wide open in terms of design freedom, so I wanted to take advantage of this.

Once I started doing some research on weather apps I found they all were very boring an consisted of the same thing so I looked into getting an idea which was more out there. Eventually I started researching the weather on the other planets of the solar system and found it was so interesting so I tried to develop that idea more.

PAPER TO SCREEN

Sketches are finshed and I'm ready to move onto screen, The first things I created was a style tile, this gave me a good reference for later in terms of fonts, colour schemes and the position for icons and titles. Next came the planets. I wanted to do these next becasue I was just quite excited about seing what they would turn out like. I experimented a bit with shading and it made them stand out so much more.

Once the planets were done I had to finsh the rest of the icons before moving onto the screens themselves, they didn't take as long as the planets. Lastly it was putting everything together on the screens and eventually putting these into invision and creating a working prototype.

I'TS ALL COMING TOGETHER

After seeing everything coming together was quite exciting, I was really happy with the way things were looking, so the next step was to bring all the screens into invision and make it look like a real working app.

This was my first time using invision, I was instantly surprised by how simple the interface was and how easy it was to create a working prototype. In terms of the prototype, it works so well in showing the user how to navigate the app.
