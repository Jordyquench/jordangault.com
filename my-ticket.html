MY TICKET

My Ticket is an app Origin built, they wanted a short video for twitter to show off the app.

I am kind of thinking now that I am moving from being just a UX designer to being a UX designer/Animator, which I am totally fine with, I am really enjoying doing these animations. But they aren't taking over in terms of design, becasue of the simple fact I am also the one designing the assets for the animations in most of the cases.

To start off with this video I needed to find out what I needed for it in terms of assets. I needed a screen cast of a runthrough of the app, some logos (My Ticket and Origin) and the dimensions. I have done videos in the past and used the wrong dimensions, trying to resize a video or animation while maintaining the quality isn't easy.

To start off I recorded the run through of the app, which wasn't as easy as it sounds, 1. I have never used the app before so I need to make sure I get everything in that they want to show, and 2. It cant be too long or the video itself will be too long to keep anyones attention. Once I had that recorded I took it into Adobe Premiere and cut it up before exporting that in clips and bringing it into After effects to start to add aniamtions. I created a mask around the screen of an iPhone x and places the video clips within that and made it look like there was a user using the app. Once everything was in place and lloked good I added the logos and exported the video.
