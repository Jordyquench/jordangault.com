BRIEF

This project was to create an infographic on population, it could be interpreted in many different ways, I didn't want to do something simple tho, for example the population of a ceratin country.

I actaully came across my idea by accident, I was reading a news article about this neighborhood in Syria and how people were being escorted across nearly the whole country to get away from the war. So I thought it would be a cool idea to base my info graphic on the population change in this Al Wa'ar neighborhood from 2013 - 2017

PAPER TO SCREEN

The characters that I sketched out were one of the things I was looking forward to making the most, so I did them first. I changed the designs around a few times because I had a design in my head and I also wanted them to be unique.

While designing the characters I was also coming up with the colour scheme, it is quite a dull colour scheme, which might sound weird but I wanted a brown tone to represent the location and situation.

The idea for the infographic was turning out better than I hoped, the information and story was working really well and adding the characters and the icons I created were really bringing it all to life.

I'TS ALL COMING TOGETHER

So all of the ions are done now, it is time to put them all together. Initially I wanted to use Dr. Noble more but I don't think it would look right, so I decided to just use him on the home page.

The colour scheme and all of the icons fit well into the overall style that I was aiming for. The age of user I was aiming for was kids in school around 13-17, I think I got this right in terms of the style and how they are going to learn about the Noble gases.
