MY FIRST WEEK

First week is nearly over, I am going to try and do these posts as often as I can. I want to keep track of how I am getting on while on this placement year and by doing this I can look back and see what I learned and what I worked on. I also want to use the posts as references for my essay I have to write at the end of the year.

As I said before I had kinda already met the people in the office but this is the first proper meeting and I can already tell I am going to struggle with names, they all seem really nice though. In terms of the work they have got me doing, the first few days I have been doing email designs for Danske Bank (sounds pretty cool saying I have designed emails for such a well known company). Well to be fair the emails I designed have already been done before, it is just getting me use to guidelines and stuff. I also used Sketch for the first time in about a year and I can already tell it is going to be a program I will want to get use to.

After the first couple of days I got my first new email to design which I thought would have felt quite daunting but it really didn't, I was looking forward to getting stuck into some real client work. then I found out I have to build the emails as well... and that was so confusing when I first looked at it, I have found a way to try and work it out though. I have cut each section of code up on an existing email and I am trying to use it as like a master/apprentice exercise.

The first day of trying to build these emails didn't go so well but today I got it! It is getting use to the fact that they are written useing tables and not HTML5, the only reason they are done like this is because the way in which Danske Bank run the emails it can only read tables, or something like that, I am no developer I don't know how emails run.

Another thing I have had a look at this week are Remus Uomo emails, they are done using Mailchimp, which seems a lot different from the Danske Bank ones but I havn't actually had a go at doing those ones yet.
