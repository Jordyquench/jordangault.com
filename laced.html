BRIEF

This was a short masterclass that I did at the beginning of my second year in University, it was a very wide brief, create a micropublication on something you think could be a real life product.

When I first read this I didn't really know where I could go with it, it could be anything. At this point I was very much getting into trainers (I still am but this was when it started) so I knew that I wnated to do something around this.

PAPER TO SCREEN

After I had some ideas down I needed to refine one and get the final idea started. I knew for sure I wanted to do something about shoes but at the start I was aiming towards an app that people could buy and sell shoes but that was already a thing so I tried to come up with something different and eventually came up with the idea of custom shoes.

The next problem was the name of the app, I intially had Laced Up but that is a thing already too, there is a very small gap in the market for what I am trying to do here, the name Laced wasn't taken though and my idea was far enough away from what Laced up was I decided to go for that.

The final idea I came up with was that the user can either put in a shoe they already own and want to customise or they cn choose from a library of shoes and customise one of those pairs. The user will also be able to post their shoes on a timeline for other people to see.
