WHY AM I STARTING A BLOG?

As I said at the top of the page, I am not someone who has ever wrote a blog. The idea came to me while I was writing a journal for my time on placement, I was thinking that it could be a better idea to have it all on one place. Especially for when I am writing my end of year essay for my time one placement.

While initially the blog was created for the sole purpose of having a place for my placement journal, it could also be a place for me to document my progress on any side projects I am currently working on.

I might also wrote some posts on anything I find interesting I find online that could be useful or any events I attend.

Placement

View my journal about how my placement is going. Here I am am going to be making regular posts on what I am doing and how I am learning.

Side Projects

I want to start making posts about my side projects. I have a few ideas for projects that I want to start, some of which will be client work and some that are just for fun.

Events/Other

There are always design event and conferences happening, I was to make it to as many of these as possible and document how it goes.
