BRIEF

The brief for this project was to create a website about elements from the periodic table, I decided to base mine around the noble gases and I aimed it towards kids.

The reason I wanted to stick to the noble gases was that I thought they would have a lot of options when it came to the style that I would eventually use. What made mw want to aim it at kids was the illustrative aproach I wanted to take when designing the website and the icons.

PAPER TO SCREEN

The idea of aiming this towards kids was really important in the design process, I really wanted to go with a illustrative style for the website because at the time of this project I was really getting into illustration I just wanted to do more of it.

Starting out with the logo and the character were the first things that I created. I was trying out some new things with these, the ideas for this style came from wanting to make a more children friendly website that helps them learn about the periodic table.

I'TS ALL COMING TOGETHER

So all of the ions are done now, it is time to put them all together. Initially I wanted to use Dr. Noble more but I don't think it would look right, so I decided to just use him on the home page.

The colour scheme and all of the icons fit well into the overall style that I was aiming for. The age of user I was aiming for was kids in school around 13-17, I think I got this right in terms of the style and how they are going to learn about the Noble gases.
